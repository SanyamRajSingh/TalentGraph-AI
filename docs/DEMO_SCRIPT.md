# 2-Minute Demo Script

## 0:00-0:15: Open The Dashboard

Open `http://localhost:3000`.

Point out the five-step workflow:

1. Generate Role DNA
2. Generate Candidate Twins
3. Evaluate Candidates
4. Rank Candidates
5. View Explanations

## 0:15-0:35: Generate Role DNA

Paste `data/demo/job-description.md`.

Click **Generate Role DNA**.

Show:

- role title
- fingerprint
- required skills
- radar chart
- deterministic reasoning

## 0:35-0:55: Generate Candidate Twins

Paste one or more resumes from `data/demo/resumes/`.

Click **Build Digital Twin** for each candidate.

Show:

- candidate metrics
- skills
- timeline
- reasoning

## 0:55-1:15: Evaluate Candidates

Click **Evaluate Match**.

Show:

- overall match
- confidence
- technical, growth, domain, and culture cards

## 1:15-1:30: Switch Hiring Personas

Use the persona selector in the ranking panel.

Show how the leaderboard reflects persona-specific weighting.

## 1:30-1:45: View Explanations

Click **Generate Explanation**.

Show:

- strengths
- risks
- why this candidate
- counterfactual improvement cards

## 1:45-2:00: Export XLSX

Click **XLSX** in the ranking panel.

Open the workbook and show:

- rank
- candidate name
- score
- confidence
- strengths
- risks
